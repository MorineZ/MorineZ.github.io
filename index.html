Heqin Zhu (朱河勤)

Heqin Zhu is a Ph.D. student at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), supervised by Prof. S. Kevin Zhou. He received his bachelor degree from USTC in 2020 and master degree from Institute of Computing Technology (ICT) and University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) in 2023.

His research areas are medical image analysis and bioinformatics, with specific focus on RNA structure prediction, anatomical landmark detection and few-shot learning.
